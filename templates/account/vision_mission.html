Vision and Mission

Vision and Mission:
Atheist Society of Nigeria’s vision is to promote a secular Nigeria where public policy, education and scientific enquiry will be founded on sound reason, rationality and evidence, and not influenced by religious beliefs. Atheist Society of Nigeria’s mission is to work with state and non-state institutions to promote and protect the rights and interests of atheists, freethinkers and nonbelievers in the country and also undertake educational and advocacy projects.
I affirm that I support atheistic goals and purposes and also support the vision and mission of Atheist Society of Nigeria
