Social Events

When we get together
The Atheist Society of Nigeria will hold national conventions every year in states in Nigeria. Our convention will bring together atheists, agnostics and freethinkers from around the country to celebrate Science, Reason and Atheism.

The date of our first convention will be announced shortly!

Upcoming Meetups

Apart from the annual national convention, members of the association hold meetups to socialize and catch up with one another. Though the meetups are informal, we like to keep a list for people who want to join to find out when they will hold somewhere convenient for them.
