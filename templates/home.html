Welcome

Welcome to Atheist Society of Nigeria website, We are registered with the corporate affairs commission and have a mandate to promote a secular Nigeria where public policy, education and scientific enquiry will be founded on sound reason, rationality and evidence, and not influenced by religious beliefs. We provide a sense of community to all nonreligious and non-theistic Nigerians and work with state and non-state institutions to promote and protect the rights and interests of atheists, freethinkers and nonbelievers in the country.
