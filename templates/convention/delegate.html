CALL FOR STUDENT DELEGATES SPONSORSHIP APPLICATION

The Atheist Society of Nigeria (ASN) in collaboration with the International Humanist and Ethical Union (IHEU), is calling for applications from students who want to attend the national convention from their various schools.

We will be sponsoring 20 students from 10 schools who meet our criteria for selection.

Sponsorship details include transportation to and from Lagos and modest accommodation for two days in Lagos.

Interested students should fill this form with a scanned copy of a valid means of student identification. And a short compelling post as to why you should be considered for sponsorship to the convention.

Students who apply are expected to establish local chapters of the ASN in their various higher institutions after the conference in line with objectives of the organisation.

Students can alternatively send applications to info@atheist.org.ng in PDF format in a one page attachment, with all the informations as contained in this form.

Application closes on 14th of October 2017.
